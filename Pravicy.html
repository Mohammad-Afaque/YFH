Privacy Policy

IDENTITY AND RESPONSIBLE ADDRESS

The security and privacy of our clients' personal data are top priorities for us at YFH. In order to improve our services, we carefully gather, use, and retain data while always adhering to Mexican data protection regulations. We employ strict security procedures to protect your data during your interaction with us, and your information will never be disclosed to outside parties without your permission.

In compliance with the provisions of the Law on Protection of Personal Data Held by Private Parties, YOUR FUTURE HOME S. DE R.L. DE C.V. (MEXICO), Y F H HORIZON REAL ESTATE L.L.C (U.A.E.) and PT. YOUR FUTURE HOME BALI (INDONESIA), doing business as YOUR FUTURE HOME, hereinafter referred to as “THE RESPONSIBLE PARTY,” makes this Privacy Notice available to the HOLDERS of personal data, agents, and any other person involved. The purpose of this notice is to inform them of the treatment that will be given to the personal data collected, used, stored, transmitted, and/or transferred by these companies doing business as YOUR FUTURE HOME

1. What personal data do we collect from you?

As a borrower, user, buyer, consumer, or client of any of our goods and services that we market, we may request personal information from you, which varies depending on the case, such as:

  • Your name, nationality, date and place of birth, occupation, and address
  • Your email address and telephone number(s)
  • Your financial data, such as bank accounts, credits, movable and immovable property, assets, liabilities, among others
  • Information about your spouse, ancestors, descendants, heirs, legatees, and beneficiaries Official documents that prove your identity and the information you declare, such as your Tax ID number, ID number, passport number, etc
  • Additionally, in compliance with various regulations, you may be required to fill out and sign various forms with personal information as required by banking, trust, notarial institutions, and authorities, to provide the notices that the authorities may require under the applicable legislation.

2. What do we use your personal data for?

This company collects and uses your personal data to fulfill the following purposes:

  • To confirm your identity
  • To understand and meet your needs in order to provide better service, such as selling apartments or real estate services, construction, remodeling, tourism, and/or housing services that our company is currently or in the future marketing, among others.

3. With whom do we share your information and for what purposes?

Your personal data is only handled by the company’s staff (and when appropriate, by credit institutions or credit bureaus, notaries, real estate brokers, and/or governmental institutions) in order to provide the services or sell the goods you request. Therefore, your personal data is not transferred to any third party, except to fulfill legal obligations before the competent authorities, whether judicial, administrative, and/or tax authorities, including public records and notarial or cadastral files.

4. How can you limit the use or disclosure of your personal information?

You can limit the use and disclosure of the personal information you provide to this company through the following means we have implemented:

  • Presenting your request in person at our office, addressed to the person in charge of privacy at this company
  • Sending an email to the following electronic address: contact@yfhinternational.com

5. How to access, rectify, cancel, or oppose the processing of your personal data?

You may exercise the rights of access, rectification, cancellation, and opposition or revoke your consent by submitting a request through the means indicated above. It is understood that once they are reflected in the service or sales contracts, you may not exercise any of the aforementioned rights over them, only over those retained in the company’s database.

6. How to know about changes to this privacy notice?

This privacy notice may undergo modifications, changes, or updates, which may be consulted on our website: www.yfhinternational.com.

7. How to contact us?

If you have any questions regarding this privacy notice, you may address them to:

  • Email: contact@yfhinternational.com
